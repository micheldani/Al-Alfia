ABOUT HARINSA QATAR

Harinsa was founded in Qatar in 2007, with the aim of bringing its world-class expertise and know-how in construction and design development to the local market. A fully-owned Qatari contracting company specialized in low and high-rise buildings, the company has cemented its position and reputation in the local construction market, with a diversified portfolio of close to QAR 5 billion.

Today, this portfolio spans large-scale developments in Qatar such as the lifestyle Dream Hotel, Yasmeen City projects, the JW Marriot and Waldorf Astoria 5-star hotels. It has also grown to include a number of military and sports facilities, civil works, schools, and data center projects, among others. Harinsa’s competitive edge in the market has built on its value creation for clients, notably in high-end 5-star hotel turnkey construction.

Beyond construction, Harinsa’s ambition is to create a collaborative environment that values project team engagement and attracts and empowers top talent in the field.
