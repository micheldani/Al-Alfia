ABOUT BONNET À POMPON

Bonnet à Pompon is a celebration of children’s innate styles, expressiveness, and authenticity. Created in 2009 as a children’s fashion brand, Bonnet à Pompon children’s clothing line ranges from 0 through to 12 years. All collections are designed in Spain and produced in Spain and Portugal.

Bonnet à Pompon’s manufacturing process ensures that each garment is carefully crafted to the highest quality standards; the brand works with original fabrics as well as unique prints from Italy, France and other European countries.

Bonnet à Pompon’s exclusive and distinct style revives the “bohemian chic” spirit, yet exhibits an elegant touch of French inspiration. With uncompromising quality and attention to detail, the brand’s style exudes authenticity, with exquisite designs and exclusive colors that ooze a sweet, relaxed, and attractive energy that naturally connects with children. These designs guarantee pure comfort with 100% natural materials such as cashmere, linen and cotton.

The garments are the beginning and the main axis of all collections. From the very first sketches through to packaging, Bonnet à Pompon’s garments are a labor of love, dedication and perseverance. The brand is ever growing and expanding throughout Europe, Mexico and the Middle East.
