ABOUT PETROSERV LIMITED

Established as a construction company in 1978, Petroserv expanded its services over the years to become one of the leading infrastructure, oil and gas, and building contractors in Qatar. Capitalizing on a large specialized pool of technical talent, as well on world-class plant and equipment fleets that are supported by skilled service mechanics, Petroserv delivers optimal performance, creating value for clients through timely and professional project delivery.

Always at the forefront of industry innovation, the company was one of the first to identify and launch the facility management service in Qatar in alliance with a Portuguese listed company, becoming one of the most successful asset managers in the country.
