ABOUT Oriental Trading Company

Established in 1957, Oriental Trading Company (OTC), an ISO-certified oil company in Qatar, is among the nation’s largest and longstanding players in the field. Building on its high-quality offering, world-class talent, and goal-oriented growth philosophy, the company today represents a broad spectrum of multinational powerhouses in the energy sector.

Across Qatar, OTC’s portfolio covers mechanical and electrical products, service workshop and on-site services, construction equipment, plumbing materials, as well as a water testing laboratory for the public and private sector and for the supporting oil and gas industry.

Today, OTC continuously invests in value-adding acquisitions to broaden its scope of activities and expand its clients’ businesses. Whether through cutting-edge workshop facilities, innovative technologies, or product diversification, OTC has become the preferred supplier across growth industries – particularly construction – growing and serving the market with state-of-the-art, high-quality, and user-friendly products and solutions.
