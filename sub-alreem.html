ABOUT AL REEM GARDENS

Strategically located in the fast-developing growth corridor of Al Rayyan Municipality, just five minutes west of the Education City and directly opposite to the Al Rayyan FIFA 2022 Stadium and Mall of Qatar, Al Reem Gardens is a fully integrated villa compound developed by Al Alfia Holding, stretching over a land area of 200,000 square meters. The development, opened in 2018, consists of 504 villas separated into two compounds within the overall site. It offers a wide range of facilities for each compound, including a clubhouse; basketball, tennis, squash and soccer courts; a nursery, mosque, supermarket, and beauty lounge; individual green park gardens and play areas; a hi-tech surveillance system; and on-site property management and maintenance services.
