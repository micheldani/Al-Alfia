ABOUT QALCO

Qatar Lubricants Company (QALCO) is the only manufacturer of lubricants, greases, specialty product and third-party contract blending and storage services in Qatar. Since 1997, QALCO has been a leading manufacturer, distributor, marketer, and owner of the only blending plant for premium lubricating oils and greases, specialty products, and chemicals. It has equally established itself as a leading provider of energy-related services in Qatar.

Today, QALCO’s portfolio boasts some of the largest companies in the automotive, industrial, marine, oil exploration, and production fields across Qatar’s public and private sectors. QALCO’s footprint has also fast expanded to cover 12 destinations around the world.

Through state-of-the-art technologies and best-standard practices in the lubricants industry, QALCO has earned a name for product quality, diversity, and flexibility. Attesting to its high-quality technology and reliability standards, the QALCO plant is certified with ISO 9001 for Quality Management and ISO 14001 for Environmental Management.
