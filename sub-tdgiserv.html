ABOUT TDGISERV Services

TDGISERV is a facility management company, established under a partnership between Portugal-based TDGI and Qatar-based Petroserv. The business specializes in facility management, its main scope covering operation and maintenance works for buildings, compounds, as well as technical, industrial, and medical equipment and facilities.

Building on its wide-ranging expertise and know-how, as well as multinational talent pool, TDGISERV focuses on efficiency and diversification to grow both its services and impact on the local market. The company’sstrong work ethic and diversified offering have positioned TDGISERV as the leading asset management company for many of Qatar’s biggest and most iconic properties and developments.
