ABOUT DELTA CORPORATION

Established in 1995, Delta Corporation is a design and engineering manufacturing company, specialized in the provision of solutions for the oil and gas sector.

Powered by its innovative drive and state-of-the-art manufacturing plant and techniques, Delta has built a distin-guished market offering vis-à-vis competitors, becoming the lead supplier of smart innovative equipment to some of the largest international and national oil companies. The company has also earned its name for service excellence, through competitive after-sales services and expert field installation teams.
