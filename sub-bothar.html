ABOUT BOTHAR GROUP

Operating under Bothar Group of companies, an international contractor with over 25 years of experience, Bothar Boring & Tunneling introduced tunneling methods and solutions to Qatar’s construction industry, becoming a local pioneer in the field of tunnel contracting.

The company has consistently demonstrated its commitment to innovation, having made considerable investments in R&D (Research and Development) and cutting-edge in-house technology designed and produced for the industrial sector.

Boasting a wide-ranging roster of high-profile projects that have raised the bar for production and construction in Qatar, the company has been recognized for its impressive track record, minimal environmental footprint, highly skilled talent, and diverse capabilities.

Coupling quality with an in-depth understanding of the unique challenges of each project, Bothar Boring & Tunneling has earned its place as a market-leading and innovative tunneling contractor.
